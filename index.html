I am an incoming MSc student in Statistics at the University of Toronto (starting Fall 2026). My research interests are constantly evolving but currently centre on Bayesian deep learning and uncertainty quantification, with a particular focus on distilling posterior knowledge from Bayesian neural networks into compact, deployable models.

I am also an incoming Data Science Scholar at the Data Sciences Institute (SUDS program), working under the supervision of Prof. J. Gronsbell on behaviour tracking and visitor analytics at the Art Gallery of Ontario. Previously, I completed an Honours BSc in Statistics and Finance (double major) at the University of Toronto.

Outside of research, I am a member of the Statistical Society of Canada and moonlight as a derivatives trader using ML.
